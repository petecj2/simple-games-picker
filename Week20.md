**Game Context:**
- Bills: 12-3-1, #5 seed in AFC, coming off Wild Card win vs Jacksonville
- Broncos: AFC divisional home game, Mile High altitude advantage
- **Weather**: Denver in January, altitude (5,280 feet) is a factor

---

#### **ELO Prediction: Buffalo Bills -0.5 (53% win probability)**

The ELO system shows this as essentially a toss-up with Buffalo holding a razor-thin edge.

**Key ELO Insights:**
- **53% win probability** - Barely distinguishable from coin flip
- **0.5-point spread** indicates minimal separation
- Buffalo's slight edge comes from marginally better performance against quality opponents
- Home field for Denver nearly offsets Buffalo's ELO rating

**ELO Confidence:** 4/10 (Very low - essentially coin flip)

---

#### **Betting Prediction: Denver Broncos -1.5**

The betting markets show MASSIVE reverse line movement toward Denver.

**Line Information:**
- **Current Spread**: Denver -1.5 (opened Buffalo -1.5)
- **Line Movement**: 3-POINT SWING from BUF -1.5 to DEN -1.5
- **Total**: 45.5-46.5

**Key Betting Factors:**

1. **REVERSE LINE MOVEMENT:**
   - Bills opened as 1.5-point favorites
   - Line flipped to Denver -1.5
   - Public overwhelming backing Buffalo
   - Sharp money clearly on Denver

2. **Large Sharp Wagers:**
   - Multiple six-figure bets on Denver futures ($100K, $75K, $50K at +350)
   - Denver has 24.2% of handle vs Buffalo's 16.5% of tickets

3. **Mile High Advantage:**
   - 5,280 feet elevation affects conditioning
   - Home field advantage amplified in playoffs

**Betting Confidence:** 7/10

---

#### **Analysis: Agent Disagreement - Tiebreaker Needed**

The agents disagree on this near-toss-up game.

**Why Agents Disagree:**
1. **ELO sees slight Buffalo edge**: 53% probability, -0.5 spread
2. **Betting sees sharp money on Denver**: Line flipped 3 points
3. **Both acknowledge this is close**: ELO at 4/10 confidence
4. **Reverse line movement**: Classic sharp money indicator

**Tiebreaker Analysis:**

This is a classic sharp vs. square scenario. The public sees the Bills as the more talented, playoff-tested team. But the line movement tells a different story - when the line flips from Buffalo -1.5 to Denver -1.5 despite overwhelming public support for Buffalo, that's professional money screaming Denver.

The key factors favoring Denver:
1. **Reverse Line Movement**: 3-point swing against public money is massive
2. **Mile High Altitude**: 5,280 feet affects visiting teams
3. **Sharp Money**: Six-figure wagers on Denver futures
4. **Home Playoff Atmosphere**: Broncos fans create hostile environment

The key factors favoring Buffalo:
1. **Playoff Experience**: Bills have 7 consecutive playoff appearances
2. **Josh Allen**: MVP-caliber QB in big moments
3. **ELO Slight Edge**: Model gives Buffalo 53%

**The Decision:**
Take Denver. The reverse line movement is too significant to ignore. When professional bettors move a line 3 points against 75%+ public money, they're seeing something the public isn't. The Mile High altitude advantage combined with sharp money consensus makes Denver the pick despite Buffalo's slight ELO edge.

**Injury Impact:**
- **Bills**: Jordan Poyer (S, hamstring) OUT, Ed Oliver and Curtis Samuel activated from IR
- **Broncos**: Drew Sanders (LB, ankle) OUT - significant loss at second level

---

#### **Final Selection: Denver Broncos**

**Confidence Level: MODERATE (7/10)**

**Reasoning:**
1. **Reverse Line Movement**: 3-point swing is the strongest indicator
2. **Sharp Money**: Six-figure wagers on Denver
3. **Mile High Factor**: Altitude matters in January
4. **ELO Is a Toss-Up**: Only 53% for Buffalo, easily overcome
5. **Classic Sharp Play**: When line moves against public, follow the money

**Pick:** Denver Broncos -1.5
**Expected Result:** Broncos win 24-20
**Key to Game:** Denver's altitude advantage and sharp money confidence
